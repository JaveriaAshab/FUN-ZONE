FUN ZONE Parking

Free Visitor Parking

Parking is Free at FUN ZONE.

Valet Parking

FUN ZONE provides a Valet Parking service for AED 120 available at P1 parking.

Annual Pass Holders

All Annual Pass holders enjoy complimentary parking at FUN ZONE! Valet Parking is available for a fee at P1 Parking.

Shuttles, Limousines, Taxicabs, Drop-Offs

Guest requiring bus or taxi service should make their way to the Bus and Taxi Center located next to the Riverland French Village and arrivals plaza.

Guest being dropped off with limousine and/or personal drivers should use the drop off area located adjacent to the taxi center or P1 Parking.

Vehicle Assistance

If you should experience car problems while visiting FUN ZONE, battery jump starts are provided as a courtesy to our guests. If additional aid is required, raise your vehicle’s hood if possible, and report your vehicle's location to any parking attendant for assistance.

Parking for Guests with Disabilities

Designated parking areas are available throughout FUN ZONE for guests with disabilities. A valid disability parking permit is required.

Disability Parking Lots

Guests with mobility disabilities—including those traveling with personal wheelchairs, electric scooters or other mobility devices—should utilise the VIP Car Parks disability section, located a short distance from the Main Entrance of RIVERLAND Dubai. Please note that courtesy trams are not needed, and therefore do not serve these locations.
