Park's Characters

Hover over the pictures to see the deatils about the characters.

Mickey Mouse

Mickey Mouse, the cheerful and iconic character created by Walt Disney, embarks on delightful adventures with his friends. From magical moments to heartwarming stories, Mickey embodies the spirit of joy and friendship, making him a timeless symbol of happiness for audiences of all ages.

Minnie Mouse

Minnie Mouse, the sweet and stylish companion to Mickey, brings grace and charm to every scene. With her polka-dot dresses and cheerful spirit, Minnie adds a touch of elegance to the enchanting world of Disney. Together with Mickey, she creates timeless moments filled with laughter and love.

Mario

Super Mario, the iconic plumber, embarks on daring adventures in the Mushroom Kingdom. With his trusty sidekick Luigi, Mario faces challenges, defeats Bowser, and rescues Princess Peach. Armed with power-ups like the Super Mushroom, Mario is a timeless hero in the world of video games.

Harley Quinn

Harley Quinn, the unpredictable partner of the Joker, brings chaos to Gotham City. Formerly Dr. Harleen Quinzel, she traded her lab coat for colorful mayhem, wielding her signature mallet. With a love-hate relationship with the Joker, Harley is a captivating and complex character in Gotham's rogues' gallery.

Chucky

Chucky, the infamous killer doll, terrorizes with his sinister grin. Possessed by a vengeful spirit, Chucky seeks mayhem, leaving a trail of horror. This pint-sized terror is a chilling figure in the realm of horror movies.

Genie

Genie, the magical being from Aladdin, grants wishes with boundless charm. Living in a magical lamp, Genie's cosmic powers bring joy, laughter, and enchantment to the world. A whimsical friend, he turns every wish into a dazzling adventure.

Queen Elsa

Elsa, the Snow Queen, commands ice and snow with regal grace. From Disney's Frozen, Elsa's powerful magic weaves a tale of self-discovery and sisterhood. With a heart as pure as ice, she transforms fear into love, leaving a frosty touch on our hearts.

Joker

The Joker, Gotham's chaotic maestro, dances through the shadows with a macabre sense of humor. Unpredictable and unhinged, he paints the town with chaos and laughter. A villain wrapped in enigma, the Joker is the harlequin of havoc in the dark streets of Gotham.

Olaf

Olaf, the cheerful snowman from Arendelle, loves warm hugs and adventures. His innocent joy melts hearts as he navigates through a world of ice and magic, spreading warmth wherever he goes. A flurry of fun, Olaf is the embodiment of friendship and the magic of winter.

Teletubbies

The Teletubbies, Tinky Winky, Dipsy, Laa-Laa, and Po, explore the colorful and whimsical Teletubbyland. With their unique antennae and TV screens on their bellies, they embark on playful adventures, spreading laughter and joy to children around the world. Each day is a fun-filled exploration in the magical world of Teletubbies.

Victor Van Dort & Emily

Victor Van Dort, a shy and well-meaning young man, finds himself accidentally married to Emily, the Corpse Bride, in the enchanting Land of the Dead. Together, they navigate the challenges of the afterlife, love, and self-discovery in Tim Burton's darkly whimsical tale, 'Corpse Bride.

Pennywise

Pennywise, the sinister entity that often takes the form of a clown, terrorizes the town of Derry by exploiting the deepest fears of its inhabitants, with a particular focus on children. As the embodiment of evil, Pennywise embodies the nightmarish horrors that lurk beneath the surface of a seemingly ordinary town, making it a truly chilling presence.
